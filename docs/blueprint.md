# **App Name**: Koel Group - Sustainable Textiles

## Core Features:

- Company Directory: Showcase a directory of companies operating under Koel Group with links to individual pages.
- Investment Pitch Generator: AI tool that generates customized investment pitches based on user-selected criteria (e.g., target investor type, sustainability focus, ROI expectations).
- Waste Transformation Visualizer: Visually represent the process of textile waste transformation into sustainable products using infographics and interactive elements.
- MSME Partner Portal: A dedicated section for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to explore partnership opportunities with Jayqo Textiles and access resources for sustainable practices.
- Contact Form & Information: Implement a contact form with company contact information, location map, and social media links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Royal Blue (#4169E1) for calls to action and main branding.
- Accent color: Silver (#C0C0C0) for data visualization and subtle dividers.
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) for clean and sophisticated feel.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif font for a modern and readable feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif font to ensure consistency and readability across devices.
- Use clean, modern icons related to sustainability, technology, and collaboration.
- Employ a grid-based layout with ample white space for clear and organized content presentation.
